It’s time to talk about the power behind your plug.

10 years ago people started asking how their food was made. Today we want people start asking about how their power is made.

The energy experiment

power is power, isn’t it?

It doesn’t make a difference how fast your cell phone charges, or how hot your hair dryer blows, but choosing a power supply that uses more and more smart energy sources every single day, can make a difference to the planet.

"The power industry is the biggest part of the problem of greenhouse emissions, but it has the potential to be an even bigger part of the solution."

David Crane
NRG President and CEO

Your power is your choice

In most states, you can choose the source of the power supplied to your home. It takes no time at all and can be done over the phone or online, just contact your provider for more information.

And for those states that don’t give you a choice, well, maybe it’s time they did.
